our history

As a trusted name in contract construction, we have been delivering excellence for the past five years, successfully completing a diverse range of projects. Our portfolio includes the Meenakshi Agro Chemicals facility unit-2 Balanagar, numerous individual residential homes across various locations, the Maharshi Vidya Mandir school building in Kondapur, Hyderabad, and our ongoing work with SRP Minerals Private Ltd.
